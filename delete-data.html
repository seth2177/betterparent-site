Request Data Deletion

Effective date: June 30, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your data from Better Parent, the family activity app. You can delete your data at any time — in the app or by contacting us.

Delete everything (account + all data)

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data:

  • Open Better Parent → Profile → Delete account.
  • This permanently removes your account and all of your data. It cannot be undone.

Delete some data (without deleting your account)

Inside the app you can remove specific data while keeping your account:

  • Children's profiles: Profile → Edit → remove a child.
  • Journal entries: Family journal → delete any entry.
  • Saved activities: tap the bookmark again to unsave.

Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email sethturnbo@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete my data." We will verify your request and delete your data within 30 days.

What data is deleted

Upon deletion we remove all personal data we hold for your account, including:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Children's profiles (first name or nickname, birth month and year)
  • Home ZIP/approximate location
  • Saved activities, activity ratings, and your family journal entries
  • Notification (device messaging) token

What is kept, and retention period

After deletion, no data that can identify you is retained. Anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot be linked to you may remain. Any residual copies in backups are purged within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about deleting your data? Email sethturnbo@gmail.com.
